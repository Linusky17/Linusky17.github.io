HackNA (AKA North Allegheny Hackathon)
  • Co-Organizer/Director

    Date: August 2017 - 2019

    Location: North Allegheny Senior High School (Pittsburgh, PA)

    Community Outreach: Over 200 Participants Each Year

    Description: HackNA (https://hackna.org/2019) is a middle and high school hackathon taking place annually at North Allegheny Senior High School. During the competition, teams will create a software or hardware project from scratch based on a central theme revealed on-site. No prior experience is required. Anyone interested can also attend our workshops and learn from our mentors to help you get started! At the end of the day, teams will pitch their project to a panel of judges in a chance to win sponsored prizes and rewards.

    Role Description: Organized the HackNA hackathon competition for 2 years (2019 & 2020). Coordinated every aspect of the event including finding sponsors, advertising the hackathon at different local communities, setting up food catering and hosting the actual event on site.
